# Agent Probe / Case 028 / Ukrainian Power Hero — Style-Family Spec

## Product role decision
**Case 028 should become a resilience campaign hero: a high-conviction public front page for energy support, reconstruction funding, infrastructure solidarity, or emergency civic action rather than a neutral utility website.**

This is a power-and-resistance statement surface.

---

## Why this mode fits the source
- The title directly couples **Ukrainian** and **Power**, which strongly suggests resilience, infrastructure, and political urgency.
- “Hero” indicates a forceful front-page composition centered on one major message and a few actions.
- Energy support and civic solidarity campaigns need **clarity, seriousness, and mobilization** rather than broad browsing.
- The likely composition balances **symbolic strength with practical action links**.
- This mode suits a public funding or awareness push where infrastructure is both literal and symbolic.

---

## Style-family thesis
This family works for:
- energy solidarity campaigns
- reconstruction funds
- infrastructure support programs
- emergency response initiatives
- civic resilience statements

It is good at:
- resolve
- urgency
- public trust
- call-to-action clarity

It is bad for:
- neutral corporate branding
- dense archives
- routine utility workflows
- playful commerce

---

## Non-negotiable visual locks
1. **One forceful hero statement must dominate.** The first screen should articulate the cause and the kind of support being mobilized.
2. **Action options must be concrete.** Donate, partner, supply, sponsor, or read field updates should be visible quickly.
3. **The tone must feel resilient, not sensationalized.** Strength and seriousness matter more than dramatic gimmicks.
4. **Visual symbolism should support infrastructure and solidarity.** Color, contrast, and layout can carry that weight without over-illustration.
5. **Supporting proof must be concise and credible.** Use short facts, partner markers, or update lines rather than long persuasion copy.

---

## Concept probe suggestion
**Grid Support Now** — a public campaign hero for emergency power restoration funding, combining one strong solidarity statement with three urgent support paths and one operations update.

---

## Acceptance checklist
- The page reads as a **resilience / infrastructure support hero**.
- One message clearly leads the screen.
- Action paths are visible and practical.
- The tone is serious, strong, and credible.
- Supporting proof is compact and trustworthy.
- The concept plausibly fits a real civic or humanitarian campaign.

---

## Failure tests
- **Fail if** the page becomes generic nonprofit marketing.
- **Fail if** emotional tone overwhelms clarity and trust.
- **Fail if** actions are vague or buried.

- **Pass if** the hero feels like a real public mobilization surface.
- **Pass if** strength and solidarity are visible without melodrama.
- **Pass if** the page makes a clear case for immediate support.
