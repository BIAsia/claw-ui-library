# Agent Probe / Case 001 / Always Remember the Beginning — Style-Family Spec

## Product role decision
**Case 001 should become a commemorative archive landing sheet: a memory-led opening surface for a cultural archive, memorial collection, oral-history release, or anniversary edition rather than a dashboard, commerce page, or general-purpose homepage.**

This is an entry artifact, not a browsing system.

---

## Why this mode fits the source
- The title implies **memory, origin, and reverence**, which naturally supports a commemorative rather than transactional product mode.
- The tall portrait ratio suggests a **poster or memorial-sheet composition**, not a dense application shell.
- “Always remember the beginning” reads like a **public-facing declaration** or curatorial thesis.
- The likely emotional center is **one dominant statement plus restrained supporting context**, which fits an opening artifact well.
- The reference feels suited to an **invocation / dedication layer** that frames a broader product family.

---

## Style-family thesis
This family works best for products that need to open with:
- memorial framing
- anniversary statements
- archival prologues
- campaign dedications
- public remembrance pages

It is good at creating **weight, pause, and ceremonial focus**.
It is bad for:
- comparing many items at once
- heavy navigation
- analytics or operations work
- long explanatory reading

Use it when the product needs a **single emotional premise** before the user moves deeper.

---

## Non-negotiable visual locks
1. **One dominant thesis line must anchor the composition.** The page should be readable from a distance through a single oversized statement.
2. **Portrait-poster balance must remain intact.** Even on responsive layouts, the page should feel like a tall commemorative sheet rather than a wide hero website.
3. **Supporting metadata must be sparse and ceremonial.** Dates, location, archive note, issue label, or dedication line should appear as short fragments, never paragraphs.
4. **Negative space must do real work.** Do not fill the composition with extra cards, illustrations, or utility chrome; the silence is part of the design system.
5. **Typography must carry the emotion.** The visual hierarchy should come from scale, spacing, and restraint rather than decorative components.

---

## Concept probe suggestion
**Origin Ledger** — a public memorial opener for an oral-history archive that introduces a new collection of first-person beginnings: first migration, first protest, first publication, first home.

Translate the style into a solemn opening surface with one major line, a date span, archive identifier, and one short curator’s note.

---

## Acceptance checklist
- The first read is **dedication / memorial opener**, not generic landing page.
- One line of text clearly dominates the entire surface.
- Supporting information is short, factual, and secondary.
- The page feels emotionally deliberate rather than feature-rich.
- The composition preserves a poster-like vertical dignity.
- The concept plausibly serves as the opening sheet of a larger archive product.

---

## Failure tests
- **Fail if** the page turns into a normal marketing hero with buttons, testimonials, and product blurbs.
- **Fail if** multiple equal-weight blocks compete with the main statement.
- **Fail if** the tone becomes cheerful, sales-led, or app-like.

- **Pass if** the page feels like a ceremonial first page to a memory system.
- **Pass if** the main statement can be understood before any small text is read.
- **Pass if** the implementation feels sparse on purpose rather than unfinished.
